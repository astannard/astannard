The Pitch

You don't need to pitch

Don't have a good idea, well pitch a bad one

  • 5 seconds to introduce yourself
  • 25 secs to tell the story of the problem
  • 20 secs for your solution
  • 10 secs for who you need to help you
  • And your off

Team Formation

It's all About the team - find a team of people you like as well as an idea you like.

It's a good time to try out new skills you don't have to be stuck what your doing for you're day job

Validation

  • Analytics - create a signup page explaining the 'Unique Value Proposition' and measure the click through rate. Pivot the product until your happy with the conversion rate (see buffer)
  • Survey Monkey -
    • Do people experience the problem your product solves?
    • Do they like the solution?
    • Would they pay?
  • Pound the street - not your friends

Pivoting

No one likes the concept, what to do?

Get feedback

  • Is it solving a problem no one has? Brand new product required maybe
  • Is it solving a problem, but people don't like the solution? Rework retest
  • Are you asking the wrong questions or the wrong people? Retest

Presentation
