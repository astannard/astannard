The Pitch

You don't need to pitch

Don't have a good idea, well pitch a bad one

  • 5 seconds to introduce yourself
  • 25 secs to tell the story of the problem
  • 20 secs for your solution
  • 10 secs for who you need to help you
  • And your off

Team Formation

It's all About the team - you're going to be in a pressure cooker over the weekend find a team of people you like as well as an idea you like.

It's a good time to try out new skills you don't have to be stuck what your doing for you're day job

Validation

  • Analytics - create up a signup page explaining the 'Unique Value Proposition' and measure the click through rate. Pivot the product until your happy with the conversion rate
  • Survey Monkey -
    • Do people experience the problem your product solves?
    • Do they like the solution?
    • Would they pay?
  • Pound the street - ask anyone but your mum

Pivoting

Get feedback

  • Are you asking the wrong questions or the wrong people? Retest
